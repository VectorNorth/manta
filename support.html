App Information

Manta provides access to publicly available maritime safety information, including data from the National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency (NGA) Maritime Safety Information website (https://msi.nga.mil). The app is intended for informational purposes only and should not be used as a primary navigation tool.

Always verify critical safety information with official sources and certified navigation equipment. For official maritime safety notices, visit:

  • NGA Maritime Safety Information
  • Your local maritime authority or coast guard
